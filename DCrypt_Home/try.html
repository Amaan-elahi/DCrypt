What is A Cryptic Hunt?

Cryptic hunts are online treasure hunts, you will be provided a question, maybe a hint or two and you have to find the answer, you can expect yourself to look for clues all over the internet, using ciphers, extracting metadata or just figuring out the popculture reference.
